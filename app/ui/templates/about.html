Introduction

Welcome to PII Finder! Our mission is to protect personal privacy by providing a reliable and efficient way to detect and remove personally identifiable information (PII) from your documents.

What is PII?

Personally Identifiable Information (PII) includes any data that could potentially identify a specific individual. This includes, but is not limited to, full names, email addresses, phone numbers, social security numbers, home addresses, and dates of birth.

Why Use PII Finder?

  • Accuracy: Our tool leverages cutting-edge machine learning models to accurately detect various forms of PII.
  • Efficiency: Save time by automatically scanning large documents without the need for manual review.
  • Compliance: Ensure your data handling practices are in line with privacy laws and regulations, protecting both you and the individuals in your datasets.

How It Works

PII Finder uses advanced algorithms to scan your documents and identify any information that could be classified as PII. Simply upload your document, and our system will provide a detailed report of detected PII along with options for handling each piece of information.
